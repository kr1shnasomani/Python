You are given an integer ‘n’.

Your task is determining the sum of the first ‘n’ natural numbers and returning it.

### Example:

**Input:** ‘n’ = 3

**Output:** 6

**Explanation:** The sum of the first 3 natural numbers is 1 + 2 + 3, equal to 6.


### Sample Output 1:

